The Big (English-language) Communities
Sure, size isn't everything — but there's certainly a lot to read on these communities.


Colors: Over 2.21 million colors of every hue, saturation, and brightness. Now you can paint with all the colors of the wind.

LyricWiki: Find the lyrics to more than 1.94 million songs, from old favourites to the ones you can't quite get out of your head.


Wikianswers: More than 1.08 million questions are waiting to be answered or improved. Over two-thirds are unanswered.


Speedy deletion Wiki: Over 764,000 articles that Wikipedia thought non-notable but somebody cared about.


Scratchpad: More than 239,000 pages on all sorts of topics.


Familypedia: The Genealogy and Family History Wiki: Over 234,000 articles and other useful pages about YOUR relatives.


Military history wiki: Over 207,000 articles talking about everything Military, from Tanks & Ships to Castles & Military Leaders.


Marvel Database: Imperius Rex! More than 185,000 articles on Marvel Comics and characters.


Wookieepedia: A community with more than 136,000 pages on every Star Wars character, planet and creature.


EverQuest 2: The quest continues with more than 135,000 articles about the popular MMORPG.


WoWWiki: A very popular community with more than 105,000 articles on the MMORPG World of Warcraft and other Warcraft games.


Yu-Gi-Oh!: Get your Duel on! Over 102,000 articles on the popular Yu-Gi-Oh! franchise.


DC Database: Look! Up in the sky! More than 101,000 articles on DC Comics and characters.
Vintage Sewing Patterns: More than 83,000 articles about vintage patterns, links to sellers and user reviews.


Logopedia: For you Logo Enthusiasts or those just looking for that memorable Logo or Corporate Brand, Logopedia has over 59,000 pages to help you out.


Doctor Who: The Doctor Who community has around 57,000 articles about the universe of Doctor Who.


The Ice Hockey Wiki: This community scores lots of goals, with more than 54,000 pages about hockey teams and players.


The Elder Scrolls Wiki: With over 50,000 pages, the Elder Scrolls Wiki surely will take you on a magical journey through Tamriel.


Star Wars Fanon: The galaxy far, far away gets a little bit bigger with over 49,000 pages of Star Wars fan fiction.


Memory Beta: A community about Star Trek spinoffs and other non-canon works, clocking in at over 48,000 articles.


Proteins Wiki: A community-moderated encyclopedia for proteins, with an emphasis on function and structure, with over 47,000 pages.


FFXIclopedia: Over 46,000 articles about Square-Enix's popular MMORPG, Final Fantasy XI.


Yu-Gi-Oh! Custom: Over 44,000 pages on custom cards for the Yu-Gi-Oh! series and card game.


Memory Alpha: Over 42,000 Star Trek articles that boldly go where no community has gone before.


Recipes Wiki: Over 42,000 delicious recipes to tempt your taste buds.


American Football Database: Players, Games, Leagues and all, with over 41,000 pages, American Football Database is sure to tackle you with information.
